Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Our comprehensive FAQ section provides quick solutions to common queries, from how to report an issue to tracking complaint status, ensuring that citizens can easily use Civic Eye without confusion.

How do I report a civic issue?

Simply click on "Report Issue" in the dashboard, fill out the form with issue details, upload an image if possible, and submit. Our AI will help classify and route your complaint automatically.

How can I track my complaint status?

Use your unique complaint ID in the "Track Complaints" section to get real-time updates on the progress of your reported issue.

Is my location data private?

Yes, location data is only used to route complaints to the appropriate authorities and is stored securely according to our privacy policy.
