About Civic Eye

Civic Eye is an AI-powered civic issue management platform built to make cities cleaner, safer, and smarter. We empower citizens to report issues instantly while ensuring authorities receive accurate and actionable insights.

Our Mission

To create a transparent, efficient, and responsive system for civic issue redressal using cutting-edge AI technology. We bridge the gap between citizens and authorities, making urban governance more accessible and effective.

Our Vision

Smarter, sustainable cities where every citizen has a voice in shaping their community. We envision a future where technology seamlessly integrates with civic life, creating transparent and accountable governance systems.

Our Values

Transparency, Accountability, Innovation, and Citizen Empowerment guide everything we do. We believe in open governance, reliable service delivery, and putting citizens at the center of urban development.

Proven Results

Our case studies demonstrate significant improvements in response times, citizen satisfaction, and municipal efficiency across multiple cities.

Our Story

Civic Eye was born from a simple observation: despite living in the digital age, reporting and tracking civic issues remained frustratingly analog. Citizens would call helplines, send emails that went unanswered, or simply give up on reporting problems in their communities.

Founded in 2024, our team of technologists, urban planners, and civic enthusiasts came together with a shared vision: to harness the power of artificial intelligence to create a more responsive and transparent civic governance system.

Today, Civic Eye serves multiple cities across India, processing thousands of civic issue reports monthly and helping authorities respond more efficiently to community needs.

What Makes Us Different

AI-First Approach

Our platform uses advanced machine learning for automatic issue classification, duplicate detection, and intelligent routing to the right authorities.

Mobile-Optimized

Designed with a mobile-first approach, making it easy for citizens to report issues anytime, anywhere, using just their smartphone.

Privacy-Focused

We prioritize user privacy and data security, ensuring that personal information is protected while maintaining transparency in governance.

Data-Driven Insights

Authorities get actionable insights and analytics to improve service delivery and make informed decisions about urban infrastructure.
