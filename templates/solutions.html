Smart City Solutions

Discover how AI and IoT technologies are transforming urban infrastructure

Pothole Detection

AI-powered cameras and sensors automatically scan roads to detect potholes. The system marks their exact location on a digital map and alerts the municipal team. This ensures quicker repairs, fewer accidents, and safer driving conditions for citizens.

Computer Vision GPS Mapping Real-time Alerts

Smart Garbage Monitoring

AI-powered IoT sensors and augmented reality interfaces monitor garbage bins in real-time. Smart waste management with predictive analytics, route optimization, and automated alerts for efficient collection.

IoT Sensors Mobile Alerts Analytics

Water Leak Detection

Our IoT-enabled water monitoring system detects leaks in pipelines early. Using real-time sensors, even small leaks are identified before they cause flooding or water loss. This helps in saving water, reducing costs, and preventing infrastructure damage.

Flow Monitoring Early Warning Water Conservation

Smart Street Lighting

Intelligent street lighting system that automatically adjusts brightness based on ambient conditions and pedestrian activity. Motion sensors detect movement and brighten lights accordingly, while dimming during low activity periods to save energy.

Auto Dimming Motion Detection Energy Efficient

Complaint Tracking

Citizens can track their complaints directly through the Civic Eye platform. Every complaint gets a unique ID and real-time updates (Received → In Progress → Resolved). This ensures transparency, accountability, and trust between citizens and authorities.

Unique ID Real-time Updates Transparency

AI Fraud Detection

Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze complaint patterns, text similarity, and location data to identify potentially fake or duplicate reports. This prevents system abuse and ensures resources are allocated to genuine issues.

Machine Learning Pattern Analysis Spam Prevention
